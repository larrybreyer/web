Welcome to the Unix Systems Services website.

Unix Systems Services is an IT consulting firm, established in California in 1996.
Headquartered in San Diego, with offices in Burbank, San Francisco, and Mountain View.
Unix Systems Services provides IT consulting service for all dominant flavors of Unix, including:
RedHat, Ubuntu, AT&T, Berkeley, Solaris, HP-UX, IRIX, DEC, and AIX.
Our primary focus is interoperability within the Unix and Linux families of operating systems.
