Religion: Introduction

My focus will be on monotheism as it appears to me to be the center of the strongest religions. By strongest I mean a belief system people will stake their lives on.

Religion is all about faith. It is not about being right or wrong. The atheist and the agnostic are not right or wrong. They simply don't get it. And, if you look at their arguments, you will see that their arguments are not with God; they are with Man.

About the existence of God: I hope to convince the reader the entire argument over whether God exists is specious and is based on the presumption we do not know the limitations of the word Exist. But, don't take my word for it. Look up the great French philosopher Rene Descartes who undertook the simple task of proving the Universe exists. How did he fare? He failed, concluding, "I think, therefore I am."
