Question: What is the current definition of "cancer"?

Response: According to the National Cancer Institute (NCI), the current definition of cancer is:

Definition: "Cancer is a disease in which some of the body's cells grow uncontrollably and spread to other parts of the body."

Alternative Definition: Some researchers propose a revised definition of cancer, which is:

Definition: "Cancer is a disease in which normal cells become transformed and evolving, leading to uncontrolled growth and spread."
