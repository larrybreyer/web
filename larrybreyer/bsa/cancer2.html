Q: It is my understanding the disease changes over time. Are there any models that describe the progression?

A: Yes, there are several models that describe the progression of cancer over time. Some of the most widely accepted models include:

**Hallmarks of Cancer Model**

Characteristics: Sustained proliferative signaling, evading growth suppressors, evading apoptosis, sustained angiogenesis, activated invasion and metastasis, enabling replicative immortality

  • Sustained proliferative signaling
  • Evading growth suppressors
  • Evading apoptosis
  • Sustained angiogenesis
  • Activated invasion and metastasis
  • Enabling replicative immortality

**Cancer Stem Cell Model**

Description: Cancer arises from a small population of cancer stem cells that are capable of self-renewal and differentiation into more differentiated cancer cells.

**Evolutionary Model**

Description: Cancer is viewed as an evolutionary process, where cancer cells undergo genetic mutations and selection to adapt to their environment and become more aggressive.
