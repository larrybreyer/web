As a Configuration Management Administrator I am required to assist in the support and administration of enterprise Configuration Management (CM) standard applications within Broadcom. I work in conjunction with the CM Leads on implementing enterprise Software Configuration Management (SCM) and Hardware Configuration Management (HCM) solutions for the Broadcom engineering community. I implement Broadcom standard SCM and HCM tools. I assist in implementation and support documentation to help transition groups to new tools and procedures and ensure the groups continuous success with the tools.
